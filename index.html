🔬 Olympiad Tips for Success

💡 Be Consistent

Even 30 focused minutes a day builds real results over time.

🧠 Prioritize Sleep

Your brain needs rest to retain complex concepts like mechanisms and equations.

🥦 Fuel Yourself

Eat well, hydrate, and take care of your mental health — especially during prep season.

📚 Practice Strategically

Focus on past Olympiad papers and your weak spots — not just random topics.

🤝 Ask & Discuss

Don’t be afraid to ask questions — even advanced ones. This site is for that!

🚶 Take Breaks

Walk, stretch, or even listen to music. Your productivity depends on it.
